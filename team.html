Ryusei Streetfood / Team

Meet our Team!

Our group consists of 3 home-cooks that have decided to share a bit of japanese culture with the world.
Founder of this company is Mei Suzuki, born in Yokohama, Japan. In 1990 she moved to Heidelberg where she first started out as a math professor at the University Heidelberg. Soon she came to miss her home so much that it became daily practice to cook traditional dishes from her home. After sharing a few home-cooked meals with her two friends(also home-cooks) Hiroko Schmitt and Sabrina Meyer, they decided to quit their jobs and become full-time cooks, starting with their small business "Ryusei Streetfood".
